Nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, Bhutan, officially known as the Kingdom of Bhutan, is a small, landlocked country known for its breathtaking landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and unique approach to development. Governed as a constitutional monarchy, Bhutan is bordered by India to the south and China to the north, with a population of approximately 750,000 people.
